Summary: Latitude vs. X

For this project, weather data from OpenWeatherMap for over 500 cities were gathered. Data for maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed were plotted as a function of latitude. Based on the graph, observations were made about whether or not there was a correlation between latitude and that weather parameter.
